Tagaytay

Tagaytay offers many beautiful tourist spots such as the Taal Lake and Volcanoes, Tagaytay Picnic Grove, and People's Park in the Sky. There are also many exciting activities to do there like ziplining and having fun in amusment parks and go restaurants that serve delicious food. Here are some pictures we took when we went to Tagaytay. Just make sure to bring a jacket and an umbrella. It might be sunny but it can still be cold.
